name: Banner

Banners are used to convey time bound messages, statuses and specfic actions to
our users. Depending on the timespan and importance of the message, they can
either be a permanent part of the page, or can be dismissable.

<Playground>
  <Banner backgroundColor="black5" textColor="black100">
    Placeholder message text.
  </Banner>
</Playground>

<Spacer my={2} />

## Colors

We primarily use banners that are either dark gray on light gray, or white on
black.

<Playground>
  <Banner backgroundColor="black5" textColor="black100">
    Example light banner.
  </Banner>
</Playground>

<Playground>
  <Banner backgroundColor="black100">Example dark banner.</Banner>
</Playground>

<Spacer my={2} />

When communicating a status, we use colored icons in along with text.

<Playground>
  <Banner backgroundColor="black5" textColor="black100">
    <TimerIcon top="4px" mr={0.5} fill="yellow100" /> Registration pending
  </Banner>
</Playground>

<Spacer my={2} />

In rare instances that we are communicating highly important messages we will
use colored backgrounds with white text.

<Box my={1}>
  <Banner backgroundColor="green100">Example success banner.</Banner>
</Box>

<Box my={1}>
  <Banner backgroundColor="yellow100">Example warning banner.</Banner>
</Box>

<Box my={1}>
  <Banner backgroundColor="red100">Example error banner.</Banner>
</Box>

<Box my={1}>
  <Banner backgroundColor="purple100">Example notification banner.</Banner>
</Box>

## Dismissable Banner

You can specify the `dismissable` prop when the banner should be dismissable.

<Playground>
  <Banner dismissable backgroundColor="black100">
    Please verify your email address
    <Button ml={2} variant="primaryWhite" size="small">
      Resend Email
    </Button>
  </Banner>
</Playground>
